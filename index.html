TECH ENTHUSIAST

I am a pre-final year Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) student at VITC, deeply passionate about the dynamic intersection of technology and management. My journey into the world of technology has led me to explore various domains, with a particular focus on becoming an AWS Cloud Architect. I am currently honing my skills in designing scalable cloud solutions and architectures, driven by a keen interest in leveraging cloud technology to solve real-world challenges.

In terms of programming, I am proficient in C, C++, and Python, which form the foundation of my technical expertise. Additionally, I have practical experience with Java for application development, and I am familiar with web technologies including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabling me to work effectively across different layers of software development.

Passionate about exploring new technologies and gadgets, I am constantly curious about emerging trends and innovations in the tech industry. This role allows me to combine my technical knowledge with leadership abilities, fostering innovation and collaboration within the robotics community.

As I continue on my academic journey, I am enthusiastic about further expanding my skills and making meaningful contributions to the field of computer science. My goal is to leverage my passion for technology and management to create impactful solutions that address societal and industrial challenges.
